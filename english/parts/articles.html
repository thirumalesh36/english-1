Article and it's uses

  We can say that an Article is Demonstrative Adjectives.

There are two articles:

  1. Indefinite Article (A or an)

    2. We use these two Articles for an Indefinite person or thing

    A farmer - We are representing farmers with the indefinite article so any farmer could be a farmer

  2. The

    3. We use this Article for an exact person or thing.

    The farmer - We are representing a particular farmer.

A or An

These two determined by Vowel or Consonant sounds.

  1. We use an for vowel sound.

    2. An umbrella, an hour, an honest person, an African, etc.

  2. Articlea for the consonant sound

    3. a girl, a man, a European, a university, a pen, etc.

The

It is used in singular, plural, countable nouns and uncountable nouns.

The man, the theatre, the mall, etc.

We use Definite Article 'The' for:

  1. Before the unique things

    2. The sun, The universe, The moon, etc.

    Example sentences

    1. The sun is the reason for solar energy.

    2. The moon is very bright today.

  2. Before the names of mountain ranges, groups of islands, rivers, seas, oceans, etc.

    3. The Pacific Ocean, The Ganga, The Himalayas, etc.

    Example sentences

    The Indian ocean is one of the five oceans.

    2. The Himalayas are located in India.

  3. Before the Holy and Historical book names.

    4. The Bible, The Quran, The Ramayana, etc.

  4. Before an adjective is modified by noun.

    5. Alexander the Great was born in Pella.

  5. Before the common nouns

    6. The cat, The book, The tree, etc.

    The book is my friend.

  6. Before the Abbreviations

    7. The UNO, The UAE, The USA, The WHO, The RBI, etc.

Omission Of Article

  In some cases, we should not use any article before a thing or person. This means the Article is omitted.

When we should not use Article

  1. Before the names of materials and abstracted nouns

    2. salt, milk, gold, happiness

  2. Before the plural countable nouns used in general sight.

    3. I like chocolates very much

    You can observe that we did not use any articles before chocolates.

  3. Before the names of meals.

    4. Do you finished lunch?

  4. Before the proper nouns except those referred to earlier. Like

    5. Names of people - Ram, Krishna, etc.

    Names of countries, cities, continents - Africa, India, Europe, etc.

    Names of individual mountain ranges - Mount Everest

  5. Before languages

    6. We commonly speak Telugu at home.

    English is the most popular language.

    Spanish is one of the most beautiful languages.

  6. Before school, college, church, bed, table, hospital, etc.

    7. 1. We go to church every Sunday

    2. Hospital is near to our town.
