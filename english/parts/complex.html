Complex Sentence

A Complex Sentence is a sentence that contains an Independent clause and one or more Dependent clauses.

  1. I got a job at Google company cause I studied hard.
  2. Many people joining in the club but Diana did not.
  3. I got on the train and then went to Vizag.
  4. Although I'm not good at playing soccer, I like that game.
  5. When the rain falls, crops will start growing.
  6. The bus station is near to my house but still, I went late to college.
  7. I use petrol for my vehicle whereas my friend uses diesel.
