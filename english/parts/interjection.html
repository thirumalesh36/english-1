Interjection

  It is a word used to express some sudden feeling or emotion.

Some words are used as Interjections like - Alas!, Hello!, Hurrah!, Ah!, Oh!, Hush!

Some groups of words are also used as Interjection words like - Well done! , Good gracious!, For shame!, etc.

Example Sentences of Interjection

  1. Hello! How are you?
  2. Hurrah! I got this.
  3. Ah! Have they reached?
  4. Hush! Don't make a sound in the Library.
  5. Oh! I forget to mention.
