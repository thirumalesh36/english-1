Syllable

  A syllable is a vowel sound of one or more groups of sounds. Every word is made up of syllables. The syllable is one of the essential parts of the English language.

Syllables are classifies as:

  1. Monosyllabic Words

    2. Word with one syllable

  2. Disyllabic Words

    3. Word with two syllables

  3. Trisyllabic Words

    4. Word with three syllable

  4. Polysyllabic Words

    5. Words with 4 or more than 4 syllables
