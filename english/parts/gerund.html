Gerund

  A Gerund is a form of the verb which ends with ing (word-ing) and has the influence of a noun and the verb

Simply a Gerund is a form of verb in which the word ends with ing.

When do we use Gerund?

We mainly use Gerund as 1. The Subject of a verb   2.The Object of a transitive verb   3. The Object of a preposition etc.

Examples

  1. I was reading a magazine.

    2. In the above example, the word reading is coming from the word read by adding ing

  2. They are playing a game.
  3. We are going to Chennai.
  4. He is coming from Serbia.
  5. Ramu is eating a mango.
  6. She is singing a song.
