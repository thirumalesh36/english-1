Noun and its types

  A Noun is a word that represents the name of a person, thing, or place.

Example

Damon(person), France(place), Table(thing), Lion(animal name), etc.

Noun again classifies into five types

1. Proper Noun
 A word that represents the name of a particular person or thing or place.

Seetha(particular person's name), Harry Potter(Name of a book which is a particular book from another book)

2. Common Noun
  A common noun is a common name for any things belonging to the same class or set.

Boy ( Boy is a common name to represent all boys we are not representing a particular boy with his name ), book, animal, bird, writer, table, girl, etc.

3. Collective Noun
It represents a group of persons or things.

Army, Cattle, Crowd, Birds, Mountains, etc.

4. Abstract Noun
An abstract noun is defined by the state, action, idea, or quality of the object.

Childhood, slavery, friendship, truth, happiness etc.

5. Material Noun
It represents a material or substance from which things are made

Gold, silver, milk, leather, wood, rock, stone, etc.

Example sentences for noun

1. Caroline goes to school early.

In above the example Caroline, was a Proper noun Cause we mentioned her name. And school was a Common noun.

2. John wakes up early morning and brings the Milk packet home.

In the above example John, was a proper noun and Milk was a material noun

Do you see any nouns in the above example? Just notice them.

3. The Cow from the Cattle is missing.

In the above example, Cow was a common noun and Cattle was a Collective Noun
