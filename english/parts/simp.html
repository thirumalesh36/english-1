Simple Sentence

A simple sentence is a sentence that contains only one Independent Clause.

Examples of Simple Sentences

  1. They sleep at 9'o clock.
  2. John is waiting for her
  3. I took the car.
  4. He rode his car.
  5. I am writing homework.
  6. He bought a pen.
  7. They sleep at 9'o clock.
  8. He is jogging in the park.
  9. He gets into the car.
  10. Diana watching a movie.
  11. They run away.
  12. He can play volleyball.
