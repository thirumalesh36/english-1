The Adjective

  An Adjective is a word used to classify/modify the Noun.

Ex: Krishna is an intelligent boy

In the above example,Krishna is the noun and intelligent is an adjective tell us what kind of Krishna is.

Example sentences of the Adjective

  1. I kike that boy.
  2. He always has two pens in his pocket.
  3. We have less time to study for exams.
  4. The car sustained heavy damage.
  5. He ate only a few mangoes.

Types of Adjectives

Adjectives of Quality

These Adjectives describe the type or quality of a person or thing.

Example sentences of Adjectives of Quality

  1. New York is a big city.
  2. The road is long to travel.
  3. America is a developed country.
  4. Tiger is the strongest animal.
  5. The container is heavy.

Adjectives of Number

Adjectives of Numbers tell us how many persons or things are meant.

  1. Especially I like three things about you.
  2. He knows many things about science.
  3. These are some mangoes.
  4. Most kids love cartoons.
  5. There are several types of cats.
  6. He usually sits on the first bench in school.

Adjective of Quantity

It tells us how much of a thing is meant.

Example Sentences of Adjectives of Quantity

  1. I bought some pens.
  2. I can do this all day.
  3. He eats any apple you gave him.
  4. You have no idea.
  5. Take great challenges always.
  6. The whole team played well.
  7. We got a sufficient number of players.
