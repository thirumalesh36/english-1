Past Tense And Its Types

When an action takes place in the past time then the Tense is said to be Past Tense

There are four types of Present Tense

  1. Simple Past Tense
  2. Past Perfect Tense
  3. Past Continuous Tense
  4. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

Simple Past Tense

It tells us when an action occurred in the past it is said to be a simple past.

Structures of Simple Past Tense

subject + v2

subject + didn't + v1

did + subject + v1

Example Sentences of Simple Past Tense

  1. I protected her.
  2. We played video games all night.
  3. I didn't study for the exam.
    4. .
  4. Did they play a game?
  5. We met yesterday.
  6. We didn't meet yesterday.
  7. Did she eat yesterday?
  8. He wrote a letter to me.
  9. I spoke to her.
  10. I was there when the celebrations started.
  11. Did she make this?

Past Perfect Tense

When an action is completed in the past before an action takes place somewhere at a point then is said to be Present Perfect Tense.

Structures of Past Perfect Tense

subject + had + v3

subject + hadn't + v3

had + subject + v3?

Example Sentences of Past Perfect Tense

  1. I had saved my files before the mobile crashed.
  2. He had gone out when I came into the home.
  3. He hadn't gone out when I came into the home.
  4. Had she finished the job?
  5. I had spoken to him.
  6. He was very happy because he had slept well.
  7. I had decided to go out.

Past Continuous Tense

When an action takes place in the past at a time before now and still goes on when another action occurred then it is said to be Past Continuous Tense.

Structures of Present Continuous Tense

subject + was/were + v-ing

subject + was/were + not + v-ing

was/were + subject + v-ing?

Example Sentences Of Past Continuous

  1. I was speaking to him.
  2. They were playing football.
  3. I had protected him.
  4. Was she traveling to Chennai?
  5. When I was running home, She saw me.
  6. I was writing a letter to him.
  7. When I was running to home, She saw me.

Past Perfect Continuous Tense

When an action occurred at some time in the past and continued until a specific time then ended in the past then it is said to be Past Perfect Continuous Tense.

Structures of Past Perfect Continuous Tense

subject + had + been + v-ing

subject + had + not + been + v-ing

had+ subject + been + v-ing?

Example Sentences of Past Perfect Continuous Tense

  1. I had been waiting for you.
  2. He had been working on this project for a week.
  3. Had they played cricket?
  4. she has not been speaking to him for three months.
  5. She had been watching the movie for 3 hours.
  6. I had been speaking to him.
  7. I hadn't been cooking this dish for an hour.
