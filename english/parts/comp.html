Compound Sentence

A compound sentence is a sentence that contains at lest two Independent Clauses.

Example Sentences of Compound Sentences

  1. I like this coffee, it is fantastic.
  2. It's cold in here, but there is a fireplace in the house.
  3. He doesn't like football, he likes volleyball.
  4. John wakes up early morning and does yoga.
  5. She is an intelligent girl, she can do everything easily.
  6. The kids are watching cartoons and the parents watching the news.
  7. I don't like tea, I like coffee.
  8. They went to Australia, exploring all beaches there.
  9. I didn't write my homework, so I was punished by the teacher.
