Homophones

  Homophones are the words when Pronounced, Which seem similar pronunciation with other somewords, but variation in Spelling or Meaning.

Example:

Bear and Bare have the same pronunciaton when Pronounced. But both are different in Spelling as well as Meaning.

Consider an another example Rose the past tense of rise and Rose, the flower. In this case both have same spelling and pronounciation but Different Meaning.

Some More Examples Of Homophones

  • buy, by
  • cell, sell
  • knight, night
  • write, right
  • wear, where
  • bloc, block
  • boar, bore
  • principal, principle
  • some. sum
