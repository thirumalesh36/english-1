Word Formation In English

  In any language commonly new words are created and become part of the language. There are several ways that can form a word in English.

Methods Of Forming New Words

  1. 1. Compounding :

    2. In this methodnew words are formed by the combination of two words. In Compounding words are formed by

    1. noun + noun

    ice + cream = ice cream

    note + book = notebook

    mobile + adaptor = mobile adaptor

    2. gerund + noun

    drawing + hall = drawing hall

    walking + stick = walking stick

    playing + game= playing game

    washing + machine = washing machine

    sleeping + bed = sleeping

    3. noun + adjective

    life + long

    world + famous

    sky + blue = sky blue

    4. adjective + noun

    smart + work = smart work

    short + shirt = short shirt

    round + ball = round ball

2. Clipping :

It is the method of word formation in which the word is reduce without changing the actual meaning of it.

    Back Clipping

  1. automobile = auto
  2. gasoline = gas

    3. Fore Clipping

  3. alligator = gator
  4. telephone = phone
  5. refrigerator = fridge

    6. Middle Clipping

  6. influenza = flu

    7. Complex Clipping

  7. situation = sitcom

3. Abbreviation Words :

  Even though these are abbreviations widely used as words in written language. In this word formation process, new words are formed when a word is becomes a short end.

  1. Dr. = doctor
  2. Mr. = mister
  3. VIP = very important person
  4. GOAT = greatest of all time
  5. Ltd = limited
  6. Km = kilometre
  7. pg = page
  8. Pvt = private
  9. Govt = government

4. Acronyms :

  In the word formation process, Abbreviation words are formed from the intial letters of other words that make new words. Such words are called Acronyms.

  1. PIN
  2. NASA
  3. ISRO
  4. ARM
  5. RADAR
  6. LASER
  7. NATO
  8. WHO
