Cohesive Devices or Linking Words

  Cohesive devices are such words or phrases that show the relationship between a paragraph or sentences of a text. These are also called Linking words, connectors, etc.

Where we are using Cohesive Devices

Cohesive devices are classified according to their use.

  1. To introduce an item in a sequence we are using first, then, in addition to , finally, etc. like words.
  2. To compare we use ditto, similarly, but, however, in contrast, meanwhile, etc. like words.
  3. To emphasize we use normally, certainly, in fact, definitely, exactly, etc. like words.
  4. To cause we use in addition to, last to, result to, due to, etc. like words.

Which words are used in cohesive devices?

  1. Linkers

    2.   These words describe the ideas of a text. like, therefore, and, but, etc.

  2. Pronouns

    3.   Pronouns are commonly used as cohesive devices of the text. like it, they, too, etc.

  3. Synonyms

    4.   Synonyms are also being used as cohesive devices to avoid the same word in a text.
