Homonyms

  Homonyms are the words when Pronounced, Which seem similar to other words but are in Different Meanings.

Sight, Cite, Site. Sight is related to eye vision, Cite means to show an example and Site means a place. They have the same pronunciation but are different in meaning.

Some More Examples Of Homonyms

  • whole, hole
  • able, abel
  • fair, fare
  • new, knew
  • hire, higher
  • flu, flew
  • accept, except
  • addition, edition
  • flour, flower
  • affect, effecct
  • hare, hair
  • very, vary

Homonyms can be Homophones or Homographs
