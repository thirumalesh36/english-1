Suffix

  A Prefix is a group of words or letters that are used to add at the end of the word. When we add Suffix to the word it becomes plural form o that word or it acts as a verb .

For Example, Take the word Act when we add a Suffix "ion" to it, it changed to Action.

Any time we add a Suffix to a word it tells us the verb tense of words whether the words are plural or singular

Suffix Meaning Examples
(word) -er, -or To compare or someone Taller, Faster, Teacher, Abrogator, Absquatulator, Abstracto, etc.
-able, -ible Capacity of something Credible, Audible, Unforgettable, etc.
-d, -ed To form past tense Added, Loved, Escaped, Enterd, Captured, Cared
-ment The state or result of an action Statement, Government, Appointment, Achievement, Excitement
-ive Having the quality of Creative, Generative, Compitative, Effective, Directive, Reflective
