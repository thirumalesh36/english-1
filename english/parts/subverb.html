Agreement between Subject and Verb

  A verb must agree with its Subject in singular or plural. This agreement is known as the Subject-Verb Agreement.

Rules for agreement of subject and verb :

  1. A singular subject should take a singular verb and a plural subject takes a plural verb.

    2. Example

    He runs quickly

    They run fastly

  2. When two are more singular subjects connected by And should take Plural verb.

    3. He and I are ready to go outside.

  3. When words connected to singular subject by With, as well as, by which, along with do not affect the verb.

    4. Damon along with his brother are waiting outside of the theatre.

  4. When the singular subject is separated by or, neither nor, either or, should take a plural verb.

    5. Either teacher or friend was able to help me.

  5. When collective nouns are treated as single the verb is singular

    6. The crowd is so rude when I was in there.

  6. Some singular nouns take a singular verb.

    7. The laptop in my office is so expensive.
