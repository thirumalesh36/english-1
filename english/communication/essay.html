Essay Writing

  An essay is a piece of content that is written from the view of the writer. I assume that you have been experienced in your school or college writing an essay. You may don't know at that time how to write an essay properly. So these are some tips that will improve your essay writing skills.

1. One theme
Your Essay must be clear and develop one theme. The idea of your essay must be clearly defined in the mind and viewed throughout
2. Sequence
The essay should follow a certain logical order throughout and come to a definite ending. For example, if you write about a person's biography mention all his childhood moments at once. but don't mention them randomly with his/her other moments.
3. Short
There is no rule for essay length but if you write a school essay it should be about three hundred words. Depending upon your topic the essay length will define. But an essay should be a brief exercise.
4. Style
The style of an essay must be Dignified, Literary, and More Formal.
5. Own Versions
We already mention that an essay is a view from a writer. He/She can include their thoughts in their essay.
