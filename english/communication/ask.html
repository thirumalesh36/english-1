Asking For Information

    Imagine you need to know what the time is now from a person. How did you ask? Before you ask somebody to know the time, It is important to know how to ask. When you ask for information from a friend or relatives you can use the Informal Form. When you ask a stranger, colleague, etc, you should use Formal Form. It shows how you respecting on them. So before you seek information must keep in mind this.

Asking Information In Informal Form

Example:

  • How Much Does The Car Cost?
  • Where Does It Store?
  • Where Does He Live?
  • When Does The Bus Start?

Asking for Information inn Formal Situation

  • Can You Tell Me What The Time Is Now?
  • Excuse Me, Sir Did You Know Where The Bus Station Is?
  • Pardon Me, Could You Tell Me How Much The Movie Ticket Cost?
  • Would You Mind Tell Me About Your Job?
  • Could You Tell Me How To Go To The USA?

If you need to know the directions to go somewhere. Before you ask somebody to know direction use these phrases. These will help you

  • 1. How can I get to.....?
  • 2. Wher is the........?
  • 3. How far is that from here?
  • 4. Could you help me to reach that street?
  • 5. What is the easiest way to....?
  • 6. Is the right way for Clark Street
  • 7. Where is the nearest market/theatre/post office
