Giving To Information

When someone needs information from you, You should know how to give information properly to them. Before you give information to them follow these keywords for giving better information

  • 1. Sure i will help you
  • 2. No problem
  • 3. Let me see
  • 4. It's my pleasure to answer that
  • 5. I can answer your question
  • 6. I would be happy to answer

If you are not able to answer that use these instead of just saying 'No', "I don't know".

  • 1. I'm sorry, I can't help you out
  • 2. That's beyond me, Sorry
  • 3. Sorry,I can't help you
  • 4. I would like to help you. Sadly, I don't have that information
  • 5. Pardon me, I'm not able to answer that

If you giving directions to someone these will help you when give directions to them

  • 1. Go straight A Head...
  • 2. Turn Back...
  • 3. Turn left/right...
  • 4. Cross that...
  • 5. Go along...
  • 6. The best way to reach...
  • 7. Take the first right/left
  • 8. The easiest way to....
