Note Making

  Note Making is one of the processes to make short notes that are available whenever we need them. In this process, the writer records important details from a Paragraph, Article, Or Meeting. It helps us to understand simply and recollect in a short time. A lot of information can be read in a short note. We can get a lot of information from short notes. That is why it is important to know how to make notes.

Making Of Notes

  • 1. Read the source (it may be a paragraph, article, or anything) once
  • 2. Highlight the important sentences. It may useful for keeping as headings
  • 3. Before making an actual note make a rough note
  • 4. Arrange them in a sequence for the actual note
  • 5. Don't change the main content or Message of the Source

Importance Of Note Making

  • 1. To read a lot of information in a short time
  • 2. When preparing for exams
  • 3. To keep the information available whenever we need
  • 4. To read only thre main points
  • 5. To avoid the reading of huge information
  • 6. For understanding the content in our own words
  • 7. No need to go through the entire information

Use These Tips To Make A Neat Note-Making Format

  • 1. Focus on the main points of the passage
  • 2. Ignore using long sentences as headings
  • 3. Prepare cleat, Brief and Accurate notes
  • 4. Use proper order for your points
  • 5. Do not include your understanding
  • 6. Use Abbreviations
  • 7. To make memorable use of colors, symbols,and diagrams
