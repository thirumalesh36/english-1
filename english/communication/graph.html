Graphs

 Graphs are used to represent information in a non-verbal form. Graphs are easy to understand. Even illiterate people can also understand this type of information. Generally, Companies' Data, Institution Data, and Surveys are in graph format. These Graphs are drawn by two or more variable quantities. There are different types of graphs to draw. These are the few among them.

Types Of Graphs

  • Bar Graphs
  • Line Graps
  • Area Graphs
  • Histogram Graphs
  • Dot Graph
  • Scatter Plot

How To Create A Graph?

To make a graph follow these steps. These will help you.

  1. Collect your data
  2. Draw an x-axis and a y-axis.
  3. Mark your variables on x and y axis.
  4. Pin the data on x an y axis
  5. Check whether your actual data and graph are relevant
