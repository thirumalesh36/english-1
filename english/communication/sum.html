Summarizing

  We learned about how to make notes. similarly Summarizing is also a method to write information in short notes. However, there is little difference between them. In Note Making, we are not allowed to include our understandings and we can't change the main idea of the passage

  In the case of summary, we can use our own words and understandings related to the actual content. However, some Keywords can be used.

  A Summary is always simple and easy to understand and it doesn't contain any idioms or metaphors. sayings of the actual Passage or Poem.

Instructions for Summarizing a passage

  • 1. After reading the passage completely, read it again
  • 2. Note down the important points and keywords to include in your summary
  • 3. Check whether your summary and actual content are similar or not
  • 4. Summarize each paragraph or segment in less than three sentences it makes readable

Keep these in your mind before writing a summary

  • 1. Main ideas of the passage or poem
  • 2. Identify what the passage is about
  • 3. Know the crucial details in the passage that similar to your ideas
  • 4. Write a Proper Title in your own words
