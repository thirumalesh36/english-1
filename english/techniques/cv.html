CV - Curriculum Vitae

CV is an acronym for Curriculum Vitae. This is all about someone's personal information about his or her career including education details, skills, the experience of his or her previous works, personal details, certifications,etc. It is a long summary of the person's career. It is asked for job interviews in almost all sector companies.

The more you're CV is attractive the more chances you select for a job

Use these simple techniques to make a beautiful CV

  1. Select a clear and attractive layout for your CV.
  2. Start a CV with your name, contact information and a photo (if required).
  3. Include your educational details in a reverse chronological order.
  4. Put a heading for every important thing.
  5. Add you're relevant skills.
  6. Highlight your skills.
  7. Add your work experience in the previous.
  8. Use formal language.
  9. Conclude with a cover letter.

Resume

A resume may be defined as one page summary of someone's information that needs to apply for a job. Every company ask resume to hire a candidate.

Use these techniques to make your resume beautiful

  1. Select clear and beautiful layout for the resume
  2. Mention your name and conatanct information.
  3. Put your educational details.
  4. Mention the skills that are required for the company you are selected.
  5. Include your additional information like hobbies, languages, etc.
  6. Add your work experience.
