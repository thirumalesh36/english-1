Paraphrasing

  The word paraphrasing means rephrasing a sentence that is someone written or spoken by using different words to understand much more easily.

Here are some steps to Paraphrasing

  1. Change the actual words by using synonyms or something
  2. Change the structure of the sentence. change the Subject in the sentence
  3. Alter Passive and Active voice
  4. Remove extra words that are not so important
  5. If actual words are complicated you can generalize them

Advantages of paraphrasing

  1. For academics
  2. To rewrite an article, essay, or something
  3. To understand much more clear
  4. Good practice for writing articles for newspapers or writing a blogs
  5. Good skill to learn
