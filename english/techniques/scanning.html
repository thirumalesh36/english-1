Scan For An Information

  Scanning is a method used for speed reading. This is a very important skill to learn especially for students. In scanning we only look for Specific information without reading everything. For instance looking for a match score in newspapers, formulas of maths in textbooks, important historical event names/date/names of kings or queens, etc.

Scan a book/material/article

  Scan when you need to know about specific information for an oral presentation or something you have to use.

  Scan when you have less time and need to read more.

Follow these steps carefully to scan

  1. Select a good material/book
  2. Look index page first
  3. Arrange the specific information based on your comfortable like alphabetically, chronologically, etc.
  4. Point your fingers at a paragraph that you are reading to get your attention
  5. Read daily
  6. Keep in mind important topics like ancient time dates, location names, names of persons, etc.
