Para Jumbles

  Para Jumbles means, Some sentences of the paragraph are assigned in a jumbled manner . These sentences are assigned with numbers or letters. Generally, it is a question to ask in any Academic or Competitive exams. When these types of questions are to be asked we have to rearrange them in a proper way

If you want to arrange them in the correct order follow these techniques. These simple techniques may save you time in exams.

  1. Identify the begining statement of the paragraph.
  2. Find out the main idea of the parajumbles
  3. Identify the connectivities like at, so, however, but, after, precise, them, if, though, although, The statements that are started with these any of the words will never be the opening sentences. These words always refer to previous sentences.
  4. Pronouns like she, he, they, him, her, it. Used when the person or thing is talking about already has been mentioned
  5. Adjectives like clever, simpler, better. These words always hold the relation to the things such can also be helped in solving para jumbles.
