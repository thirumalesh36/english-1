Movie Review

  It is so similar to writing a review about a book. Just follow these additional steps to give a better review

  1. Watch the film completely before giving a review
  2. Mention your likes and dislikes of film
  3. Say about cast performances
  4. Do not give spoilers
  5. Mention music, dance, and editing part
  6. Mention what part seems to be good
  7. Give some reasons why to watch that film to the audience
  8. Conclude your review by giving a rating to it
