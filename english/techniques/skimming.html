Skimming

To get the core concept of the material/book without reading all words in it. Get an idea of what part is important to skimming.

Consider these tips for skimming for an important topic to read

  1. Before you start skimming Know what you want to get from the book/material. For example some person's details in it, some important events that occurred in ancient times, location names, etc.
  2. As you move your eyes down to the page move them as well side by side
  3. Separate the information on what is important and what is unimportant by knowing the mood and style of the author simply think like the author.
  4. Focus on meaningful, original material
  5. Look for the main idea of the passage
  6. Read the first sentence of each paragraph
  7. Skip when you get information in the first or sentence.
  8. You do not need to read everything if you get what you need.
