Article Writing

To write a topic about something (maybe a person, events, technology, things) in newspapers, magazines, or any publications is known as an Article Writing

  To write an attractive article, it is not so hard if you know these techniques

Follow these steps to write an article

  1. Select your topic to write an article
  2. Research your topic for information
  3. Write key points that highlight your article
  4. Arrange your points in a logical order
  5. Know your audience's interest
  6. Your introduction about your topic should be impressive to reach more audience
  7. Make sure your title should not repeat
  8. Include some images
  9. Your conclusion should give satisfaction to your audience
  10. Conclude your article by giving clarity and give your answers if any questions or remaining
  11. Focus on the introduction, key points, and conclusion
