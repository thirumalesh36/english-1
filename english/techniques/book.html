Book Reviewing

  To write a fancy review about the book, you have to know how to make it a proper and attractive review. These are the very simple ways to write a book review on your own. These will make your review fancy.

  1. First, start with a couple of lines describing what the book is about
  2. Mention what you especially liked about the book:
  3. Who was your favorite character and what makes you like
  4. What was your favorite segment?
  5. Describe anything you disliked about the book
  6. Include some of your thoughts on the book by suggesting the type of reader you would recommend the book to. Like children readers, older readers, readers who like drama/fantasy/comedy, etc.,
  7. Conclude your review by giving a Rating for the book. For supposing 4.5 out of 5, 3 out of 5 based on how much you like that
