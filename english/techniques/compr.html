Reading Comprehension

  It is so important topic for your academics. Many people waste their time answering questions from the passage. These simple techniques make you better to answer quickly for passages.

  1. First, read carefully the questions under the passage
  2. Read the passage keeping question in your mind
  3. Read atlest two times to get a proper understanding
  4. While reading the passage, Notice any sentence that is also in your question it might be an answer but not all times
  5. Your eyes and mind should work together to find answers
  6. While your eyes searching for answers your mind should keep questions and ideas telling them to search for
