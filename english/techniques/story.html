Story Writing

  To tell a proper story some practice is required. It is best to know to tell or write a story. Most people think that they know to write or tell a story but not everybody knows actually. They repeat some points all the time and that makes the story unproper.

Follw these tips to improve you story

  1. Make sure that you should have to clear idea about the plot of the story in your mind
  2. Don't omit any point of your outline given.
  3. Keep the points in order
  4. Connect the points as per the given in the outline
  5. Include any dialogue or conversation if possible
  6. Giving a Conclusion about the story is more importantthanwriting a story. Note that the whole story must end with a bit of surprise
  7. To write a title use the main character's name/incident of the story. For instance Birbal And Akbar, Our First Meet, etc.
  8. Use simple english, proper grammar
  9. Edit or Rewrite until it is as best as you can make it
