Letter Writing

  These days it is a very important skill to know how to write a Letter. Everyone has sometimes to write a letter to a friend, teacher, boss, etc.,

  If you are not able to write a letter clearly it may affect on sometimes. So writing a letter is a part of communication.

There are several kinds of Letter Forms like Business Letters, Friendly Letters, and more. All these under these categories

1. Formal Letters

2. Informal Letters

  Informal Letters are which we write to friends, family members, relatives, invitation letters (it may be sometimes Formal) .

  The main difference between them is we can write our own language, accent and friendly when we write an Informal Letter. Whereas when you write a Formal Letter you should useProper Language, Grammar, and Eloquent.

Six Points To Form A Letter

1. The Heading
The writer's address and Date
26 Gandhi Street

New Delhi 110001

10 October 2019

Note: The date may be written in any ways like

10 October 2019

10th October 2019

October 10 2019

Generally Americans Put the Month before the Day.

2. Salutation or Greeting
The form of greeting will based on you and the person you are writing

To friends and members of your family you may write like these, Dear Mother, Dear Father, My dear brother/sister, Dear uncle, Dear Ram, Dear Friend, etc.,
To business people, it will be Dear sir, Dear sirs

3. The body of the letter
This is the letter itself, We write every message here. There is a difference between writing a letter to a friend and a business person. Use these suggestions to write a letter to any person you want to
  1. Split up your letter into paragraphs unless it is very short
  2. Use simple language and short Sentences
  3. Think about what you want to say before you write a letter don't lag your letter and try to be complete
  4. Put all your points together
  5. Use correct punctuation, Like commas, semicolons, and full stops
4. Leave Taking
Use specific forms of polite leave-taking are prescribed. For instance Yours sincerely, Your sincere friend, Yours faithfully, etc. The first word of the leave-taking must begin with a capital letter
5. The Signature
It is the name of the writer. This must be placed below the leave taking.
Yours sincerely

Christine Palmer

6. The Address on the envelope
This is the address of whom you writing. Write the address on the envelope like this
Mr. Stephen

99 Hill Road

Araku

Andhra Pradesh 531151
